Two-level systems are powerful tools to study quantum fields in curved spacetimes: they can be used as measuring probes, or even as communication devices. However, these models are often only studied in perturbation theory. As a first step to investigate their nonperturbative behavior, we computed the functional renormalization group flow of a field-detector system.
